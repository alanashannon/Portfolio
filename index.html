About Me

I’m a software engineer with a passion for React, Redux, Javascript and Ruby. My current project is a Javascript app called Decomposer that lets users rewrite famous pieces of classical music and play back their creations. In the midst of Covid-19, I have also been developing a Kickstarter-inspired single-page app where users can find funding for their projects and support others as well, as I feel that it’s crucial to support those in need during these rough times.

My first passion is music. I have been playing cello since I was 7 years old and performing professionally since high school. Whether through music, software engineering, knitting/crocheting, or building things, I have always enjoyed having a creative outlet, and in particular I’ve found combining music and software engineering to be a lot of fun. These two areas have also given me an eye for small details, and I live for the satisfaction of creating something pixel-perfect and intuitively designed for a great user experience.

As a fullstack software engineer, I have developed skills in Rails, Ruby, React, Redux, Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, and PostgreSQL, among others, and am always looking to learn more!
